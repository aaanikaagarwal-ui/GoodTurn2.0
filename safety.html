🔒 Safety first

Built safe from the ground up

Every safety rule lives in the core of the platform — here is exactly how we keep volunteers protected.

Safety is built into the core of GoodTurn, not bolted on. Here is how we protect every volunteer — especially those under 18.

🏠

Verified posters only

Only residents approved by a community admin can publish jobs on the platform.

👪

Parent approves every job

Volunteers under 18 cannot be booked until their guardian approves that specific listing — no exceptions.

👁️

Guardian sees messages

For minors, a parent is a silent participant on every job thread — no private one-on-one chat.

🚫

Contact filter

Phone numbers, emails, and off-platform invites are automatically blocked in messages.

📍

Arrived + done check-ins

Guardians are notified on arrival and completion. A missed check-in triggers an alert.

☀️

Daytime jobs only

No evening or overnight tasks. Guardians can also cap distance and allowed job types.

Secure payments

Funds are held in escrow when a job is booked and released only after both sides confirm the work is complete — so volunteers are paid fairly and neighbors only pay for completed work.
