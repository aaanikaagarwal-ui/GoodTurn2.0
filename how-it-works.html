How it works

From post to payment in three steps

Simple for neighbors, rewarding for volunteers, and safe for families — here is the full picture.

Three simple steps

1

A neighbor posts a job

Verified local residents post tasks with a clear description, a fair price, and a daytime window. Every listing is AI safety-screened before it goes live.

2

A volunteer requests it

Browse the board, filter by category, and tap Request. If the volunteer is under 18, their parent is notified and approves that specific job before anything is booked — no exceptions. Adults can be booked directly.

3

Done and paid

The volunteer checks in on arrival and taps All Done when finished. Funds held in secure escrow are released once both sides confirm the work is complete.

For everyone involved

🙋

Volunteers

Find nearby jobs that match your skills, build a star rating, and get paid securely. Under-18s get parent-approved safety automatically.

🏡

Neighbors

Post a task, review applicants, accept the right helper, and fund escrow. Pay only when the job is confirmed done.

👪

Parents

Approve every job, see every message, set distance and category limits, and get arrival and completion alerts.

Safety at every step

  • Verified posters only — residents are approved by a community admin.
  • Parent approval required before any under-18 volunteer is booked.
  • On-platform messaging with an automatic contact filter.
  • Arrival and completion check-ins, with alerts for missed windows.
  • Daytime-only jobs, with guardian-set distance and category limits.
