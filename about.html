About GoodTurn

Neighbors helping neighbors

We connect people who need a hand with people who want to help — safely, transparently, and for real pay.

Our mission

GoodTurn is a neighborhood-powered platform that turns everyday tasks into real opportunity. Verified local residents post small jobs they need a hand with — mowing a lawn, walking a dog, setting up a new phone, tutoring a student, or running groceries — and people in the community step up to help.

Anyone can volunteer. You simply tell us your age when you join, and you are ready to start earning and making a difference close to home. For volunteers under 18, safety is not an afterthought — it is built into the core, with a parent in the loop at every step.

What we believe

🤝

Community first

Small acts of help build stronger, kinder, more connected neighborhoods.

🛡️

Safety by design

Parental approval, on-platform messaging, and check-ins protect younger volunteers.

💚

Real opportunity

Fair pay held in secure escrow, and reputations that grow with every good turn.

🌍

Open to everyone

Teens, students, and adults alike — anyone who wants to help can join.

How it pays off

Payments are held securely in escrow and released only when both sides confirm the work is done, so trust runs both ways. Star ratings build lasting reputations, and AI smart-matching connects the right helper to the right job. GoodTurn is more than a job board — it is neighbors helping neighbors, one good turn at a time.
