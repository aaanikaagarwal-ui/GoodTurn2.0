How it works

Simple, safe, rewarding

From post to payment in three steps — with built-in safety for younger volunteers.

1
Neighbor posts a job
Verified residents post tasks with a clear description, a fair price, and a daytime window that works for their schedule.
2
Request & approve
A volunteer taps "Request." If they're under 18, their parent is notified and approves that specific job before anything is booked — no exceptions.
3
Done and paid
The volunteer checks in on arrival and taps "All Done!" when finished. Escrow releases payment once both sides confirm.
Platform features

Everything you need, nothing you don't

✦
AI Smart Matching
Our AI scores every job against a volunteer's skills, availability, and past ratings — so the right person shows up for the right job, every time.
🛡️
AI Safety Screening
Every job listing is scanned by AI before going live. For under-18s, parents get a safety score and approve everything before a volunteer is booked.
💳
Secure Escrow Payment
Funds are held securely when a job is booked and released only after both sides confirm the work is complete.
⭐
Reputation System
Star ratings and reviews build trust over time. Great volunteers get more jobs; reliable neighbors attract better applicants.
🔒 Safety first

Built safe from the ground up

Every safety rule is in the core, not bolted on as an afterthought.

🏠
Verified posters only
Only residents approved by a community admin can publish jobs on the platform.
👪
Parent approves every job
Volunteers under 18 can't be booked until their guardian approves that specific listing — no exceptions.
👁️
Guardian sees all messages
For minors, no private one-on-one chat — a parent is a silent participant on every thread.
🚫
Contact filter active
Phone numbers, emails, and off-platform invites are automatically blocked in messages.
📍
Arrived + Done check-ins
Guardian notified at arrival and completion. A missed check-in triggers an immediate alert.
☀️
Daytime jobs only
No evening or overnight tasks. Guardians can also cap distance and allowed job types.
